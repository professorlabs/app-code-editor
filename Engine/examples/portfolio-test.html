Research Overview

I am a machine learning researcher specializing in deep learning and natural language processing. My work focuses on developing novel architectures for large language models and their applications in scientific discovery.

Current Research Focus

  • Efficient training methods for large-scale language models
  • Neural architecture search and automated model design
  • Applications of AI in healthcare and drug discovery
  • Responsible AI development and ethical considerations
